# RKL Digital — Blog

48 SEO- and GEO-optimized HTML articles plus a blog index for **RKL Digital** (rkldigital.com), the AI-first marketing agency for U.S. small businesses.

## What's in here
- **48 article pages** (`*.html`) across 7 topic clusters: AI SEO/GEO, pricing & choosing an agency, traditional SEO, web design & conversion, marketing automation & email, strategy & ROI, and content/social/SaaS.
- **`index.html`** — the blog hub, linking every post by category.
- **`assets/rkl-blog.css`** — the shared stylesheet (keep it alongside the HTML).
- **`_WRITER-SPEC.md`** — the content/template spec the posts were built from.

## Built for AI search (GEO) + conversion
- `Article` + `FAQPage` JSON-LD structured data on every post.
- A "Quick Answer" box at the top of each article (the format AI engines lift and cite).
- 400+ internal links across the cluster for topical authority.
- Free-strategy-call CTAs, FAQ sections, comparison tables, and stat boxes throughout.
- Canonical and Open Graph URLs use the **apex domain** (`https://rkldigital.com`).

## Deploying
These are self-contained static HTML files. Upload the folder to your host at a `/blog` path, keeping `assets/rkl-blog.css` in place. If your published URL pattern differs from `https://rkldigital.com/blog/<slug>`, update the `<link rel="canonical">` and `og:url` tags to match.
