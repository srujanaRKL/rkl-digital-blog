What a real AI SEO agency actually does

A genuine AI SEO partner goes well beyond keywords. The core deliverables look like this:

  1. An AI-visibility audit. They test how ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews describe — or ignore — your business today, and where competitors are winning.
  2. Direct-answer content. They restructure your pages so each opens with a clear, quotable answer AI can lift, the foundation of answer engine optimization.
  3. Structured data. They implement schema markup so AI engines parse your services, location, and FAQs without guessing.
  4. Topical authority & FAQs. They build connected content clusters and a real FAQ library that mirrors how customers phrase questions to AI.
  5. Trust & local signals. They strengthen reviews, listings, and your Google Business Profile.
  6. Tracking & reporting. They monitor your AI mentions and referral traffic and report on it — not just rankings.

Crucially, they keep your traditional SEO healthy too. The same well-structured content feeds Google and AI alike, so the two should never be separate budgets fighting each other.

The 8 things to look for

Use this as a scorecard when you talk to any agency:

  • Full-stack capability — SEO and AI/GEO under one roof, not one or the other.
  • Proof, not promises — real examples of clients cited by AI tools.
  • A clear method — they can explain what they'll do in plain English.
  • Outcome reporting — leads, mentions, and conversions, not just rankings.
  • Small-business fit — pricing and communication built for your size.
  • Transparent contracts — month-to-month, no long lock-ins.
  • US-based and responsive — someone who answers when you call.
  • They practice it themselves — ask if AI tools recommend the agency.

Realistic pricing for small businesses

AI SEO pricing varies widely, but a few honest benchmarks help you avoid being over- or under-sold:

  • Focused projects (audit, schema, an FAQ build) often run a few hundred to ~$1,000 one-time.
  • Ongoing programs for small businesses typically land in the few-hundred to few-thousand per month range depending on content volume and scope.
  • Enterprise pricing (five figures a month) is built for big budgets — usually overkill for a local or growing business.

The number matters less than what's inside it. Always ask: what exactly is delivered each month, who does the work, and how will we measure whether it's working?

Red flags to walk away from

  • Guaranteed rankings or "#1 in 30 days." Nobody controls Google or AI engines well enough to promise this.
  • Secret proprietary methods they won't explain. Real expertise can be described simply.
  • Long lock-in contracts that punish you for leaving instead of earning your stay.
  • No reporting or only vanity metrics with no link to leads.
  • No examples of clients actually being cited by AI tools.

Frequently asked questions

How do I choose the best AI SEO agency for my small business?

Look for an agency that does both traditional SEO and AI/GEO, shows real examples of getting clients cited by AI tools, explains its method clearly, reports on outcomes not vanity metrics, and offers transparent month-to-month pricing. Fit with your budget and communication style matters as much as capability.

What should an AI SEO agency actually do?

It should audit how AI tools see you today, fix direct-answer content and structured data, build topical authority and FAQs, strengthen local and review signals, and track your mentions in ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews over time — while keeping your traditional SEO healthy.

How much does an AI SEO agency cost for a small business?

Most small-business engagements run from a few hundred dollars a month for focused work up to a few thousand for full-stack programs. Beware both bargain offers that can't deliver and enterprise pricing built for big budgets. Ask exactly what's included and how results are measured.

What are red flags when choosing an AI SEO agency?

Watch for guaranteed rankings, secret methods they won't explain, long lock-in contracts, no clear reporting, and no examples of real AI citations. Honest agencies set realistic expectations and show their work.

Should a small business hire an AI SEO agency or do it in-house?

It depends on time and expertise. The basics can be started in-house, but schema, content clusters, technical work, and ongoing AI-visibility tracking usually move faster with an agency that does it daily.
